Our Story


Ravi Gupta, the leader of OPG Group of Industries, attributes much of his success to his grandfather, Mr. Durga Duttji. After the tragic loss of his first son, Om Prakash, Duttji took his first grandson under his wing, molding him into a capable businessman from a young age. His approach was reminiscent of a traditional Gurukula, allowing Ravi to blend into the world of business seamlessly.

One of the most valuable lessons Duttji imparted was the importance of learning from mistakes. "Dadaji encouraged us to make our own errors, guiding us afterwards on where we went wrong," Ravi recalls. "He taught me everything, and it’s because of him that we’ve come this far."

Through Duttji’s mentorship, Ravi not only mastered the technical aspects of the trade but also gained insight into man management and the subtleties of soft power. He honored his grandfather’s investment in him by significantly increasing the capacity at Durga Rubber Works, scaling production from 100 cycle tubes a day to an impressive 1,000.

Reflecting on his journey, Ravi states, "I have struggled in life, but I have been successful. I have no regrets. Life has been a series of entrepreneurial challenges."


Our Vision


OPG Group of Industries, founded by Late Shri Om Prakash Gupta, is one of the premier business houses of Southern India for the past five decades. The Group is at present lead by Shri. Ravi Gupta, its chairman ably assisted his son Shri. Alok Gupta. Though the group started from Tamil Nadu today it has its footprints all over India. Our Group’s heritage and vast experience has given it an excellent understanding and knowledge of India’s business landscape. In its five decade of existence, the Group’s business interests have captured varied industries such as Iron & Steel, Power, Foods and Logistics. OPG Group excels in the production and manufacturing of Steel and Steel Products in South India. The group has been setting up mini plant and Rolling Mills since 1981. And now it has Furnace Units. Sponge Iron Plant and Rolling Mill Division in its Steer Units Portfolio. The Group’s steel products are branded under OPG and are very well established and preferred brand in the market for their Quality, Price and Delivery.
