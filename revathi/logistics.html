Indian Corporate Business Centre Ltd

Indian Corporate Business Ltd operates a Containerized Freight Station at Manali close to Chennai and Ennore Ports. This facility has 3 custom bonded warehouses as well as general warehousing space both built-up and open. The company is ISO 9001 certified warehousing space of 7 lac sq.ft both open and covered. The company has also started Container Freight Stations at Mumbai and Tuticorin.

This dynamic mind-set is reflected in our motto “Reaching Out” and demonstrates a tradition of innovation which is intrinsic to every part.
